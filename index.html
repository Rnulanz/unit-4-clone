Crystals Collector!!!

Rules

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number that was provide win the game starts. You lose
    the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you, until you click on it and reveal the value.
  • Each time the game restarts the value of each crystal changes.
